The Beurer UWB70 Heated Mattress Pad

Transform your sleep routine with the Beurer UWB70 Heated Mattress Pad. Perfect for winter nights or anyone who prefers a warmer sleeping environment, this innovative pad delivers soft, even heating to help you stay cozy and comfortable all night long.

Dual Control for Separate Feet and Body Heating

Enjoy the freedom to customize your warmth with two independent LED controllers. Adjust the temperature for your feet and body separately, ensuring optimal comfort during cold weather or for those who suffer from chilly feet.

9 Adjustable Heat Levels for Customized Warmth

Choose from nine different warmth settings to find the perfect balance for your needs. Whether you're looking for a gentle warmth or a more intense heat, the UWB70 offers the flexibility to suit your comfort preference.

Auto Shut Off for Added Safety and Convenience

Designed with an auto shut off feature, the Beurer UWB70 ensures you can leave it on overnight without worry. This helps maintain a safe and energy-efficient sleep environment for you and your family.

Overheat Protection for Peace of Mind

Stay safe with built-in overheating protection that automatically regulates the temperature. This ensures your comfort is never compromised and your mattress remains protected from damage.

Soft Quilted Fabric for Comfortable Sleep

Featuring a soft, quilted design, this heated mattress pad provides a luxurious feel against your skin. The breathable and cozy fabric supports a restful night's sleep while maintaining a warm and inviting atmosphere in your bedroom.

Whether you're seeking warmth for your feet, a gentle heat for your entire body, or simply a more comfortable sleeping experience, the Beurer UWB70 is a reliable and durable solution for colder nights. It's also machine washable, making it easy to maintain and perfect for long-term use.
