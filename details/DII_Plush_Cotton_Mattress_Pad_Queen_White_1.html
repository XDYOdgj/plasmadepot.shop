DII Plush Cotton Mattress Pad - Queen Size, White

Enjoy the perfect blend of softness and support with the DII Plush Cotton Mattress Pad. Crafted for comfort and designed for durability, this mattress pad brings a touch of luxury to your bedroom setup. Its 100% cotton cover offers a hypoallergenic, breathable surface, keeping your sleep cool and refreshing throughout the night.

The 8 oz cushion filling delivers a cloud-like feel, ensuring you wake up rested and rejuvenated. Whether you're upgrading your mattress or simply looking to add a layer of comfort, this pad is made to fit all queen-sized mattresses with a size of 60x80". Its flexible, stretchable design ensures a secure fit even on mattresses up to 22" deep, preventing slippage and keeping you comfortable all night long.

With its easy care features, the DII Plush Cotton Mattress Pad is machine washable, making maintenance a breeze. It's the ideal choice for those who value a comfortable, supportive, and stylish sleep environment without compromising on practicality.
